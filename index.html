Welcome to Flamencool

  • About us

    Flamencool is a learning centre for Flamenco founded in 2019 in Arnhem by a Spasnish couple who have spent a decade doing research on Flamenco music, songs, and dance. We offer a wide range of courses and services, such as tailored-courses for groups or individuals, from beginners to advanced level either in dance, flamenco technique, guitar, or singing. Flamencool is more than a school - it is an experience, a lifestyle and also a place where you can feel at home due to the high quality customer service.

  • Dance classes are focused on teaching a sequence to later adapt the rhythm, the guitar, and finally the singing, working also on the improvisation. We teach all palos of flamenco (seguirillas, soleá, soleá por bulerías, alegrías,...). Dance to the rhythm of glory, pain, and joy!

    View our Dance course

  • Flamenco serves one master, el cante. The guitar must follow the singer and the dance is the spinal cord of the song. In flamenco, the song is the ultimate expression of art, and within it are its secrets kept. In order to know these secrets, Flamencool proposes individual lessons.
